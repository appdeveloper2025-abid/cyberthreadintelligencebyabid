🚨 Common Threats

🦠 Malware

Malicious software designed to damage, disrupt, or gain unauthorized access to computer systems.

  • Viruses: Self-replicating programs that attach to other files
  • Trojans: Disguised malicious programs
  • Ransomware: Encrypts files and demands payment
  • Spyware: Secretly monitors user activities

🎣 Phishing

Fraudulent attempts to obtain sensitive information by impersonating trustworthy entities.

  • Email Phishing: Fake emails requesting credentials
  • Spear Phishing: Targeted attacks on specific individuals
  • Whaling: Attacks targeting high-profile executives
